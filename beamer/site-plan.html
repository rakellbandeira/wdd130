Overview

Purpose

This website will advertise Beamer as Design Agency to online prospecs. It will expose the main pieces of its portfolio, educate the client or reinforce the importance and impact of design and how the dynamic of the service works. The website will provide various informations for contact, as well.

Audience

The audience includes owners of small to medium businesses or their managers or other officers, as it is commom for them to intermediate the contact and decisions about the design projects they are responsible for. It's high likely that they will come looking for efficient service to save time and money for the company schedule.
